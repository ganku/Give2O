About Us

Single use plastics are wreaking havoc on the plannet. Give2O is an attempt to inspire the world to reuse and reduce our carbon foot-print. It also helps your well being by helping you track your water consumption. Everytime you drink water that is not from single use bottle, track it under this app. Our sponsors donate $1 for every 8 glasses of water tracked by the app. Stay hydrated, track reusable water consumed, help feed the needy.
